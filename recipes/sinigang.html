Ingredients

  • Sinigang Mix

Steps

  1. Cut Pork ribs to idividual ribs
  2. Add the pork to a big pot and add water. Boil the water
  3. Boil the pork ribs until tender and remove the foam on top
  4. add the sinigang mix
